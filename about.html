About Us

Growers is a Colorado owned and operated soil and nutrient company, and has been since we started in 2015. Our products are crafted with Colorado hands and hearts. After 15+ years of experience as growers, we know how to make a consistent and effective line of soils and nutrients the Colorado way – with the environment at the top of our minds.
